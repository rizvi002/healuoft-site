About

HEAL UofT is a student-run, non-profit group at the University of Toronto. We support students exploring careers in healthcare — medicine, dentistry, nursing, optometry, research, and beyond — through events, workshops, and mentorship.

Our values: Accessibility, Community, Mentorship, and Growth.
